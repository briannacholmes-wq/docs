---
title: "Themes and colors: personalize your Our World look"
description: "Choose from six preset color themes or set your own primary and accent colors. A live preview updates instantly so you see every change before you save."
---

Our World lets you personalize every visual detail — from the header gradient to the accent highlights throughout the app. You choose your theme during onboarding (Step 5), and can update it any time from the **Settings** tab. A live preview updates as you pick colors, so you always know exactly what your app will look like.

## Preset themes

Six curated themes are available. Each combines a primary (header) color with an accent (highlight) color:

| Theme | Primary | Accent |
|---|---|---|
| Plum & Rose *(default)* | `#4A2C6B` | `#C4748A` |
| Deep & Sage | `#1A3A4A` | `#7BAE8E` |
| Navy & Sky | `#2C2C5E` | `#6BAED6` |
| Mahogany & Gold | `#5C2D2D` | `#D4A84B` |
| Violet & Orange | `#3D1A5C` | `#FF8C42` |
| Forest & Lime | `#1C3B1C` | `#A8D878` |

Tap any color swatch to instantly apply it to your live preview.

## Custom colors

If none of the presets feel quite right, you can set your own colors:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open theme settings">
    During onboarding Step 5, or in **Settings**, scroll to the **Custom colors** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your primary color">
    Tap the **Primary (header)** color swatch. This controls the app header and key UI elements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your accent color">
    Tap the **Accent (highlights)** color swatch. This controls badges, active nav labels, and interactive highlights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the live preview">
    The mini app preview updates in real time — check how your header, task cards, and navigation look together.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  High contrast between your primary and accent colors makes the app easier to read. Try a dark primary with a warm or bright accent for the most legible result.
</Tip>

## Naming your child's space

During onboarding Step 5, you can also give your child's section a custom name — for example, *"Senna's World"* or *"Explorer Zone"*. This name appears as a personal label in the child's area of the app.

<Note>
  Theme changes apply immediately across the whole app, including the parent dashboard, child view, and navigation elements. You can change your theme as often as you like — all your family data stays intact.
</Note>
