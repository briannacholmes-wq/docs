The **Settings** tab on your parent dashboard is where you manage everything you configured during onboarding — your names, app name, AI guide, companion, theme colors, and notification preferences. Changes take effect immediately and are saved locally to your device.

## What you can change

| Setting | Where set originally | Where to update |
|---|---|---|
| Parent & child names | Onboarding Step 1 | Settings tab |
| App name | Onboarding Step 2 | Settings tab |
| AI guide name & personality | Onboarding Step 3 | Settings tab |
| Companion type & name | Onboarding Step 4 | Settings tab |
| Theme colors | Onboarding Step 5 | Settings tab |
| Child's world name | Onboarding Step 5 | Settings tab |
| Approval windows | Settings tab | Settings tab |
| Notification preferences | Settings tab | Settings tab |

## Approval windows

The approval windows setting controls when Our World notifies you that your child has completed a task and is waiting for your review. See [Approval Windows](/customization/approval-windows) for full details.

## Notification preferences

In the **Settings** tab, under the approval windows section, you can choose how you'd like to be notified:

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| 📱 Push | Push notifications to your device |
| 🔔 In-app | Notifications shown inside the app |
| 📧 Email digest | A summary sent to your email |
| 🔕 Silent | No notifications; check manually |

Tap any option to toggle it on or off. Multiple options can be active at the same time. Tap **Save settings** to apply your choices.

<Note>
  All settings are saved locally on your device using your browser's storage. Clearing your browser data or switching devices will reset your settings. See [Offline Mode & Data Storage](/help/offline-mode) for more details.
</Note>
