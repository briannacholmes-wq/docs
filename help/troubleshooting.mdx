---
title: "Troubleshooting Our World: fixes for common problems"
description: "Step-by-step fixes for common Our World issues: data gone after a browser change, tasks stuck on waiting, companion not growing, and blank screen on load."
---

If something isn't working as expected in Our World, the solutions below cover the most common issues. Because Our World stores everything locally on your device, most problems are related to your browser's storage or a cached version of the app that needs refreshing.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The app is showing onboarding again even though I've set it up">
    This happens when your browser's local storage has been cleared, or you've opened the app in a different browser or private/incognito window. Our World saves all setup data to the browser where you first configured it.

    **Fix:** Open the app in the same browser you used during setup. Avoid using private/incognito mode.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My child's task is stuck on 'Waiting ⏳'">
    This means your child has marked the task done, but it hasn't been approved yet. Tasks stay in "Waiting" status until you approve them — they don't expire.

    **Fix:** Go to your parent **Home** tab, find the task, and tap **Approve**. If the task shows "Approve" instead of "Review!", it hasn't been marked done by your child yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The companion isn't growing">
    The companion's happiness grows by 12 points each time you approve a task. It only updates when approvals happen — completing tasks without approving them has no effect on the companion.

    **Fix:** Approve pending tasks from your parent **Home** tab. After each approval, check the companion card to see the happiness bar increase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Theme colors didn't save after I changed them">
    Theme changes apply immediately but are saved as part of your app state. If the app restarts and the old colors appear, the state may not have saved correctly.

    **Fix:** Go to **Settings** (during the onboarding customization step or via the Settings tab), reselect your preferred theme, and tap **Save settings**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The breathing meditation overlay won't close">
    Tap the **End session** button at the bottom of the overlay, or tap the **✕** button in the top-right corner. The session runs continuously until you explicitly close it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The app isn't loading or shows a blank screen">
    This is usually caused by a cached version of the app that needs to be refreshed.

    **Fix:**
    1. Refresh the page (pull down to refresh on mobile, or press F5/Cmd+R on desktop).
    2. If that doesn't work, clear your browser cache for this site and reload.
    3. If the problem persists, try opening the app in a fresh browser tab.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications aren't working">
    Our World's notification preferences (Push, In-app, Email digest) are set in the **Settings** tab. Make sure at least one option is toggled on and you've tapped **Save settings**.

    For push notifications, your device and browser must grant notification permissions. Check your device's notification settings if push notifications aren't arriving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  If you're experiencing an issue not listed here, try refreshing the app first — most transient problems are resolved by a simple reload.
</Note>
