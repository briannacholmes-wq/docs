Our World is a Progressive Web App (PWA) built to work offline. Once you've opened the app for the first time, it caches everything it needs to run without an internet connection — no account, no server, no sync required. All your family's data lives on your device.

## How data is stored

Our World saves your family's data directly in your browser's storage — no account or server involved. It stores:

- **Your family data** — names, tasks, memories, star count, companion level, settings, approval window, and theme colors
- **Your setup status** — a record that onboarding has been completed so the app opens to the profile selector on your next visit

This means your data is:
- **Private** — never sent to any server
- **Persistent** — survives app restarts and device sleep
- **Browser-specific** — tied to the exact browser and device you used

## Installing the app

You can install Our World to your home screen for the best offline experience:

<Tabs>

## Avoiding data loss

<Warning>
  Because all data is stored locally in your browser, the following actions will permanently erase your family's data: clearing browser history or site data, uninstalling the browser, switching to a different browser, or using private/incognito mode.
</Warning>

To protect your data:
- Always open Our World in the same browser on the same device
- Avoid clearing browser site data for the app
- Do not use private or incognito windows

## Offline functionality

After the first page load, Our World caches everything it needs so it runs fully offline. All features work without a connection:

- Completing and approving tasks
- Adding memories
- Running calm and breathing sessions
- Updating settings and themes
- Earning stars and growing the companion

<Note>
  There is no cloud backup or cross-device sync. If you get a new device or switch browsers, you will need to go through onboarding again. Your previous data cannot be transferred automatically.
</Note>
