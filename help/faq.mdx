---
title: "Our World FAQ: answers to your most common questions"
description: "Answers to the most common questions about Our World — from setting up profiles and earning stars, to managing data and changing your guide or theme."
---

Find answers to the most common questions about Our World below. If you don't see your question here, check the [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) page.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to create an account?">
    No. Our World requires no account, email address, or password. Everything is stored locally on your device. Just open the app, complete onboarding, and you're ready to go.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my child use the app on their own device?">
    Our World is a single-app experience designed to be shared on one device. The profile selector ("Who's opening the app?") lets both parent and child access their own space from the same device. There is no separate child account or device sync at this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I switch between parent and child mode?">
    Tap the **Switch ↕** button in the top-right corner of either the parent or child dashboard. This returns you to the profile selector screen where you can tap the other profile.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I close the app mid-session?">
    Your data is saved automatically as you use the app. Approving a task, logging a mood, or saving a memory is saved immediately. When you reopen the app, everything will be exactly as you left it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my AI guide?">
    Go to the **Settings** tab on your parent dashboard. You can update your guide name and personality there. Changes take effect immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does my child earn screen time?">
    Each task your child completes and you approve adds screen time. The formula is: **stars earned × 5 minutes**. So a 3-star task earns 15 minutes of screen time. The current screen time total is shown in the orange pill in your child's header.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the companion growth stages?">
    Your child's companion grows through five stages as tasks are approved. Each approval adds 12 happiness points (out of 100). The five stages are: 🌱 Seedling → 🌿 Sprout → 🌸 Bloom → 🌺 Flower → 🌳 Tree. The companion's status message also updates: "Needs some love today", "Growing strong", or "Thriving and happy!".
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my app name or theme after setup?">
    Yes. Go to the **Settings** tab on your parent dashboard to update your app name, theme colors, guide, and other onboarding choices at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add a memory?">
    In the **Memories** tab on your parent dashboard, tap **+ Add moment**. Fill in the title (required), story, date, and categories, then tap **Save**. Your child can also tap **+ Add a moment** from their Memories tab, and it will appear in the shared feed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my data be lost if I clear my browser?">
    Yes. Our World stores all data in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data, switching browsers, or using a private/incognito window will result in data loss. See [Offline Mode & Data Storage](/help/offline-mode) for more details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
