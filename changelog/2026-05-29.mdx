## Bug fixes

- **Quest completion no longer crashes.** Tapping the **Finish** button to mark a quest as done now works reliably without crashing the app. Kids can finish their quests and send them for approval without interruption. See [Quests](/features/quests).
- **Smoother finish animation.** The bounce animation that plays when a child taps **Finish** has been polished for a more satisfying, responsive feel.
