title: "Parent calendar: weekly view, events, and lunch plan"
description: "Use the parent calendar to view a scrollable week, see color-coded daily events, and track your child's lunches from Monday through Friday."
---

The calendar in Our World gives you a quick, clean view of the week ahead without requiring you to switch apps. Open it from the **📅 Calendar** tab in the bottom navigation of the parent side. You'll find your week laid out horizontally, your events for the day listed below it, and a lunch planner for the whole week underneath that — all on one screen.

## Week view

At the top of the calendar card, a row of day pills shows the current week from Monday through Sunday. Each pill displays the abbreviated day name and the date number.

- **Today's pill** is highlighted in your app's primary color (purple by default) with white text, so it's immediately obvious where you are in the week.
- **Days with events** show a small colored dot below the date number.
- Scroll the row left or right to scan the week. Tapping a day pill is a quick way to orient yourself, but the event list below always reflects the full day's schedule.

## Events

Below the week row, events for the day appear as individual rows. Each event shows:

- A **color-coded dot** on the left that lets you distinguish events at a glance — for example, school drop-off might appear in rose, a work block in plum, and your guide check-in reminder in sage green
- The **start time** in bold, displayed in a compact format (e.g., 8:00a, 4:00p)
- The **event title** describing what's happening

Sample events shown in the app give you a sense of the kinds of things you can track:

| Time | Example event |
|------|--------------|
| 8:00 AM | Child school drop-off |
| 10:00 AM | Work block — grants review |
| 4:00 PM | Guide check-in reminder |
| 6:30 PM | Child pickup + dinner |

<Note>
  The calendar currently displays a fixed set of sample events to illustrate the layout. Editable calendar entries are not yet available in this version of the app.
</Note>

## Guide check-in reminder

One of the default events in the calendar is a **guide check-in reminder** in the late afternoon — shown at 4:00 PM in the sample data. This is a cue built into your day to pause and open the guide card on the home dashboard. Even thirty seconds of writing to your guide before the after-school rush can help you stay grounded.

<Tip>
  The 4:00 PM slot is intentional — it sits between your work day and the busy pickup-to-bedtime stretch. Use the guide check-in as a brief reset before re-entering family mode.
</Tip>

## Lunch planner

Below the events card, the lunch planner shows what's on the menu for each day of the school week. Each row lists the day abbreviation on the left and the planned meal on the right.

The sample lunch plan included in the app shows:

| Day | Meal |
|-----|------|
| Mon | Turkey + apple slices |
| Tue | Pasta salad + grapes |
| Wed | PB&J + carrots 🥕 |
| Thu | Chicken wrap + strawberries |
| Fri | Hot lunch day ✅ |

<Note>
  Like the event list, the lunch planner currently displays sample data to show you what the feature looks like. The ability to edit meals directly in the app is not yet available in this version.
</Note>
