Parenting is demanding, and Our World's Calm tab is built specifically for you — not just your child. It offers six guided mindfulness sessions you can start in seconds, plus a rotating "Today's Insight" card with practical, research-backed parenting tips to help you show up grounded every day.

## Your calm activities

Tap the **Calm** tab in the bottom navigation to access your six sessions. Each one is designed for different moods and time constraints.

| Activity | Duration | Voice |
|---|---|---|
| 🫁 4-7-8 Breath | 3 min | Female voice |
| 🌊 Body Scan | 5 min | Male voice |
| 🕯️ Reset Ritual | 2 min | Female voice |
| 🌿 Grounding Walk | 10 min | No voice |
| ☁️ Cloud Thoughts | 4 min | Female voice |
| 🔔 Tibetan Bowl | 6 min | Instrumental |

## Starting a session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Calm tab">
    Tap **Calm** (🌿) in the bottom navigation bar on your parent dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an activity">
    Tap any activity card. The session opens immediately in a full-screen overlay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the breathing guide">
    The animated breathing ring expands and contracts to guide your pace. Follow the on-screen instruction for each phase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="End when ready">
    The session cycles continuously. Tap **End session** whenever you're ready to return to the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The breathing phases

The guided breathing overlay uses a four-phase cycle based on the 4-7-8 breathing technique:

| Phase | Instruction | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Breathe in | Inhale slowly through your nose | 4 seconds |
| Hold | Hold still, be gentle | 7 seconds |
| Breathe out | Let it all go slowly | 8 seconds |
| Rest | Rest and be here | 4 seconds |

<Note>
  The breathing ring expands during inhale and hold phases, and contracts during exhale and rest. Each session cycles through these phases continuously until you tap **End session**.
</Note>
