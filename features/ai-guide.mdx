Your AI guide is a gentle, non-judgmental presence on your parent home dashboard. It greets you with a personalized message each day — referencing your child's name, your companion, and any tasks waiting for your attention — and gives you a private space to think out loud. You choose your guide's name and personality during onboarding, and can update it any time in Settings.

## Choosing your guide

During onboarding, you pick from four guide personalities (or create a custom name):

| Guide | Emoji | Vibe |
|---|---|---|
| Lumis | 🌙 | Calm & grounding |
| Sage | 🌿 | Wise & steady |
| Nova | ✨ | Bright & energizing |
| River | 💧 | Flowing & intuitive |

You can also type any name you like to create a fully custom guide. The name you choose appears throughout the app — in the guide card header, toast messages, and personalized greetings.

## Where your guide lives

Your guide card appears at the top of your **Home** dashboard as a gradient card labeled **"[GuideName] · Your Guide"**. Each day it shows a message personalized to your current situation — for example:

> *"Hey Brianna 🌙 Senna's morning routine is waiting for your approval. A quick tap releases her first stars of the day — and Mochi is waiting to grow."*

The message draws from your family's names and any pending tasks, so it always feels relevant.

## Sending a message

Below the guide's message is a text input where you can share what's on your mind.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the input field">
    Tap the **"Talk to [GuideName]..."** field at the bottom of the guide card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your thoughts">
    Write whatever you're feeling — your guide is there to listen without judgment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Send">
    Your guide responds with an encouraging message like: *"That makes sense. You've got this 🌙"*
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The guide's responses are supportive prompts designed to acknowledge your feelings and encourage you. The guide card is only visible in the parent view — your child cannot see it.
</Note>

## Changing your guide

Your guide is set during the onboarding flow (Step 3). You can update your guide name and personality at any time through the **Settings** tab on your parent dashboard.

<Tip>
  If you're unsure which guide to choose, start with **Lumis** — the calm, grounding default. You can always switch later without losing any of your app data.
</Tip>
