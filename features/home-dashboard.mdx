Every time you open the parent side of Our World, the home screen gives you a complete picture of your family's day at a glance. Your AI guide greets you with a personalised message, you can check in with how you're feeling, and your child's tasks are right there waiting for your attention. Nothing is buried — everything you need to keep the day moving is in one place.

## Your AI guide card

At the top of the home screen, your guide — Lumis, Sage, Nova, River, or the custom name you chose during setup — sends you a short, personalised message. The message takes into account what's happening in your family's day, such as a task waiting for your approval or a note about your child's companion.

Below the message is a text input where you can write anything on your mind: a question, a worry, something you want to think through. Type your message and tap **Send**, and your guide responds with a brief, grounding reply.

<Note>
  Your guide's name and personality are set during onboarding. You can change your guide at any time from the **Settings** tab.
</Note>

## Mood check-in

Below the guide card is a **How are you feeling?** card with six mood chips. Tap the one that best matches where you are right now. Only one mood can be selected at a time.

| Mood | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| 😮‍💨 Overwhelmed | A lot is happening and it feels like too much |
| ✨ Grounded | Calm, present, and steady |
| 😴 Tired | Running low on energy |
| 🔥 Focused | In a productive, clear-headed state |
| 💛 Grateful | Feeling appreciation for something or someone |
| 🫶 Present | Fully here, not distracted |

When you tap a chip, your guide quietly notes it. A brief confirmation appears at the bottom of the screen.

<Tip>
  You don't need to log your mood every day. Use it when you want to — even just pausing to name how you're feeling can be grounding on a hard morning.
</Tip>

## Your child's tasks

The third card on the home screen shows all of your child's tasks for the day. Each task displays:

- The **task name** (for example, "Morning routine ☀️" or "Reading 20 min 📖")
- The **star value** — shown as one, two, or three gold stars (⭐) — indicating how many stars your child earns when you approve it
- An **action button** on the right showing the current status

### Task statuses

Each task can be in one of three states:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Approve">
    The task has been assigned but your child has not yet marked it done. The button reads **Approve** with an orange tint. You can still approve a task directly from the parent side without waiting for your child to mark it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Review!">
    Your child has tapped the task and marked it done on their side. The button reads **Review!** and pulses gently to draw your attention. This is the most common state you'll act on — it means your child is waiting for you.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Approved ✓">
    You have approved the task. The button reads **Approved ✓** in green and is disabled. The task row appears slightly faded with a strikethrough on the task name, indicating it's complete.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Approving a task

Tap **Approve** or **Review!** on any task to approve it. As soon as you do, three things happen for your child:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stars are awarded">
    Your child's star total increases by the number of stars the task is worth. A confirmation toast appears at the bottom of your screen showing exactly how many stars they earned — for example, "⭐ Senna earned 2 stars!"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Screen time is added">
    Your child's screen time balance goes up by 5 minutes for every star earned. A 2-star task adds 10 minutes; a 3-star task adds 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The companion grows">
    Your child's virtual companion gains 12 happiness points. Over time, the companion visibly evolves — from a seedling to a flourishing plant, for example — as tasks are completed and approved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigating the parent side

The bottom navigation bar gives you access to all five sections of the parent app.

| Tab | What's there |
|-----|-------------|
| 🏠 Home | The dashboard described on this page |
| 📅 Calendar | Your week view, events, and lunch planner |
| 🌿 Calm | Guided breathing and mindfulness sessions for you |
| ⚙️ Settings | Approval windows and notification preferences |
| 💛 Memories | Your family's Memory Keeper |

Tap **Switch ↕** in the top-right corner to return to the profile selector and switch to your child's side of the app.
