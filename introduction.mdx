## What Our World does

Our World gives every family member their own dedicated space within a single shared app:

- **Parents** get a home dashboard with an AI guide, task management, mood tracking, a weekly calendar, mindfulness tools, and a memory keeper.
- **Children** get a fun, age-appropriate experience with daily quests, a growing virtual companion, calming activities, and shared family memories.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your family">
    Complete the 5-step onboarding to enter your names, customize your app, choose an AI guide, pick your child's companion, and select a theme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your profile">
    Each time you open the app, tap either the parent or child profile to enter your personal space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign and complete quests">
    Parents assign daily tasks. Children mark them done. Parents review and approve — releasing stars and screen time rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build memories together">
    Log special moments in the memory keeper, practice mindfulness, and watch your child's companion grow alongside them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Our World saves all your family's data locally on your device. No account creation or internet connection is required after the initial setup.
</Note>
