---
title: "Parent and child profiles: how they work in Our World"
description: "Our World uses two separate profiles — one for parents, one for children — so each person gets a space designed for them. No passwords required."
---

Our World keeps parent and child experiences entirely separate within the same app. Every time the app opens, it shows a profile selector so the right person can step into their own space. The parent profile is built around guidance, tasks, and reflection; the child profile is built around fun, quests, and growing a virtual companion. Switching between them takes a single tap.

## Selecting a profile when opening the app

Each time you open Our World, you'll land on the **"Who's opening the app?"** screen. It shows your app name at the top and two profile cards below it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="See the profile selector">
    The selector appears automatically every time the app is opened. Your app name is displayed at the top, followed by the two profile cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap your profile">
    Tap the card that matches who's using the app right now. Each card shows the person's name and a short description of their space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your space">
    The app opens directly into that profile's home screen — no password, no extra confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The two profiles

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Parent profile">
    The parent profile card shows the 🌸 icon, your name, and the label **"Your guide & settings"**.

    Once inside, you have access to five sections via the bottom navigation bar:

    - **Home** — Your AI guide's message, a mood check-in, and your child's task list for the day, including approve buttons for completed tasks
    - **Calendar** — A weekly view with events and a lunch planner for the week
    - **Calm** — Guided breathing and mindfulness sessions for you, plus a daily wellbeing insight
    - **Settings** — Approval window preferences (when you want to be notified that tasks are waiting for review)
    - **Memories** — A log of family moments you've captured, filterable by category
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Child profile">
    The child profile card shows your child's chosen companion emoji, their name, and the label **"Quests, companion & calm"**.

    Once inside, your child has access to three sections:

    - **Quests** — Daily tasks assigned by the parent, each worth a number of stars. Tapping a quest marks it done and sends it to the parent for approval. The star bank and screen-time display sit at the top of this screen, along with the companion card showing the companion's name and happiness level.
    - **Calm** — Calming activities designed for children, presented as scrollable cards
    - **Memories** — Shared family memories, viewable from the child's space
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Switching profiles mid-session

You don't have to close and reopen the app to switch profiles. A **Switch ↕** button appears in the top-right corner of both the parent and child home screens. Tapping it returns you to the "Who's opening the app?" profile selector, where you can tap the other profile to continue.

<Note>
  Our World doesn't use passwords or PINs. Profiles are open by design so that switching between them is quick and low-friction — right for how families actually use a shared device. If your family needs access controls, consider using your device's built-in screen time or guided access features.
</Note>
