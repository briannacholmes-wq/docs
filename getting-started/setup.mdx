Setting up Our World takes just a few minutes. The onboarding walks you through five short steps: entering your names, naming the app, choosing an AI guide, picking your child's virtual companion, and selecting a theme. When you're done, you'll land on a finish screen that shows a summary of your choices before you enter the app for the first time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your names">
    The first thing Our World asks for is your name and your child's name. These appear throughout the app — in greetings, on the profile selector, and in your child's space.

    - **Your name** — how the app addresses you as the parent
    - **Your child's name** — used to personalise your child's profile and companion step

    Both fields are required before you can continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your app">
    Give the app a name that feels like yours. This name appears on the profile selector screen every time someone opens the app.

    Some ideas from the app itself: **Our World**, **The Nest**, or something like **Senna & Me**. You can see a live preview of the name — including both profile cards — update as you type.

    <Tip>
      Pick something that feels warm and familiar. Your child will see this name every time they open the app.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your AI guide">
    Your AI guide lives in the parent home screen and offers reflections, encouragement, and a space to think out loud. Pick one of the four presets or type a custom name.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Lumis" icon="moon" href="/features/ai-guide">
        Calm & grounding — a steady, soothing presence for busy or overwhelming days.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Sage" icon="leaf" href="/features/ai-guide">
        Wise & steady — thoughtful and measured, good for reflection.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Nova" icon="sparkles" href="/features/ai-guide">
        Bright & energizing — uplifting and motivating when you need a lift.
      </Card>
      <Card title="River" icon="droplet" href="/features/ai-guide">
        Flowing & intuitive — gentle and adaptive, follows your mood.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    If none of these feel right, type any name you like in the **Or type your own** field. Your guide's name shows up in the parent home screen and in your child's finish screen summary.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your child's companion and name it">
    Your child's companion is a virtual friend that grows as your child completes quests. Choose from six options:

    | Companion | Emoji |
    |-----------|-------|
    | Plant     | 🌱    |
    | Cat       | 🐱    |
    | Dragon    | 🐲    |
    | Fox       | 🦊    |
    | Bear      | 🐻    |
    | Star      | ⭐    |

    After selecting a companion, give it a name — for example, **Mochi**, **Blaze**, or **Luna**. The companion's name and emoji appear throughout your child's space.

    <Note>
      The companion name field is required. You won't be able to continue until you've entered a name.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a theme and name your child's world">
    Pick the colours that will appear in the app header, highlights, and accents. Six preset colour pairs are available:

    - Plum & Rose
    - Deep & Sage
    - Navy & Sky
    - Mahogany & Gold
    - Violet & Orange
    - Forest & Lime

    A live mini-preview updates as you select each preset so you can see exactly how it will look. If you'd prefer something different, use the **Custom colors** pickers to choose your own primary (header) and accent (highlights) colours.

    Finally, enter a name for your child's space — for example, **Senna's World** or **Explorer Zone**. This is the name given to the child's side of the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens on the finish screen

After completing all five steps, you'll see a finish screen that confirms your choices:

- Your name and your child's name, each in their own profile card
- Your chosen AI guide name
- Your child's companion emoji and companion name
- A colour strip showing your selected primary and accent colours

A button reading **Enter [your app name]** takes you into the app. The first thing you'll see is the profile selector — "Who's opening the app?" — where you or your child can tap your profile to enter your own space.

<Note>
  Our World stores all your family's information directly on your device using your browser's local storage. There is no account to create, no password to remember, and no data sent to any server.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Everything you choose during setup can be adjusted later. Open the parent profile and go to **Settings** to update your approval window preferences and other options.
</Tip>
